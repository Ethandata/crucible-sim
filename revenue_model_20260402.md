## 3. Path to $1,000 MRR

### Phase 1 — First Dollar (Weeks 1–4)
- **Goal:** 1 paying customer at any tier
- **Action:** Convert 1 pilot from the 19-prospect pipeline
- **Target MRR:** $199–$499
- **Key metric:** Pipeline → Pilot conversion rate

### Phase 2 — Cover Burn (Weeks 4–8)
- **Goal:** 2–3 customers covering tool budget
- **Action:** Close 2 Growth-tier customers from top prospects (LangChain, CrewAI, Relevance AI)
- **Target MRR:** $499 × 2 = $998 ≈ break-even
- **Key metric:** Pilot → Paid conversion rate (target: 40%)

### Phase 3 — $1k MRR (Weeks 8–12)
- **Goal:** $1,000+ MRR — survival confirmed
- **Scenarios:**
  - 5× Starter = $995 (near miss)
  - 2× Growth + 1× Starter = $1,197 ✅
  - 1× Enterprise = $1,499 ✅ (single customer risk)
- **Recommended mix:** 1× Enterprise + 1× Growth = $1,998 (2× buffer)
- **Kill trigger avoided:** MRR growing week-over-week by Week 8

### Revenue Milestones
| Week | Target MRR | Customers (Growth mix) | Milestone              |
|------|-----------|------------------------|------------------------|
| 4    | $499      | 1× Growth              | First dollar           |
| 8    | $998      | 2× Growth              | Break-even             |
| 12   | $1,998    | 1× Enterprise + 1× Growth | Survival confirmed |
| 24   | $5,000+   | 1× Enterprise + 4× Growth | Growth phase       |

## 4. Tool Recommendations (Under $50/wk)

### 🥇 Recommendation 1: Hunter.io — $49/mo (Starter)
**Best for:** Sales agent (SALES-001)  
**What it does:** Finds verified email addresses for any company domain. 500 searches + 100 verifications/mo on Starter plan.  
**Why now:** We have 19 qualified prospects. Hunter unlocks direct outreach to VP Eng / Head of AI contacts without manual research. ROI: 1 closed deal at $499 = 10× monthly cost.  
**Cost:** $49/mo → fits within Sales $36/wk budget ($144/mo — needs $5 reserve tap or Sales reallocation)  
**Adjustment needed:** Sales budget = $36/wk = $156/mo. Hunter at $49/mo = 31% of budget. ✅ Fits.  
**SpendRequest required:** Yes — $49/mo, blocking for outreach.

### 🥈 Recommendation 2: Beehiiv — $0 (Launch plan) → $42/mo (Scale)
**Best for:** CMO agent (CMO-001) — content + newsletter  
**What it does:** Newsletter platform. Launch plan is FREE up to 2,500 subscribers. Scale plan ($42/mo) unlocks monetization + custom domain.  
**Why now:** A Crucible AI benchmark newsletter positions us as the authority on AI agent reliability — builds inbound pipeline, establishes thought leadership, and costs $0 to start.  
**Cost:** $0 now (Launch plan) → $42/mo when >2,500 subscribers  
**SpendRequest required:** No — start free immediately.

### ❌ Not Recommended This Week:
- **Apollo.io** ($49/mo): Overlaps with Hunter; Apollo better for larger outbound volume (500+ contacts). Revisit at Week 8 when pipeline needs refilling.
- **Buffer** ($18/mo): Lower priority — social scheduling useful but not revenue-critical in Week 1. CMO can post manually until $1k MRR.
