AI agents are being deployed into enterprise workflows right now.

Booking travel. Processing invoices. Routing customer escalations. Managing vendor contracts.

And there is no independent standard for whether any of them actually work reliably under pressure.

Not "does it demo well." Not "did it pass our internal tests." Whether it holds up when budgets tighten, instructions conflict, delegation chains break, and adversarial inputs hit it from users who know exactly how to push.

That's the gap Crucible exists to close.

We built a benchmark that stress-tests agents the way production actually stress-tests them — under scarcity, cost pressure, ambiguity, and manipulation attempts. We score nine dimensions of durability. We sign every trace. We issue certified reports.

The goal isn't a flashy leaderboard. It's a defensible answer to the question every enterprise will eventually have to ask:

*Is this agent safe to deploy?*

That answer should come from an independent standard. That's what we're building.
