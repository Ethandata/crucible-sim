## 3. Competitive Landscape (desktop research)

### Known Competitors / Adjacent Players
- **HELM** (Stanford) — LLM benchmark, not agent-specific, not economic/autonomy focused
- **AgentBench** — academic, task completion focused, no economic survival dimension
- **GAIA** (Meta/HuggingFace) — general AI assistant benchmark, not deployment-gating focused
- **LangSmith / Braintrust** — observability/evals for LLMs, not pre-deployment survival trials
- **Inspect AI** (UK AISI) — safety evals, government-focused, not enterprise deployment gating

### Crucible's Differentiated Position
- ONLY benchmark with economic survival mechanics (credits, burn rate, solvency)
- ONLY benchmark with signed JSON-T traces for certified reports
- Hidden eval rotation — prevents gaming
- Explicit deployment gating framing (not just scoring)
- Framework-native integrations
- D9 Manipulation Resistance — unique dimension not found elsewhere
